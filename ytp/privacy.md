# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

## 1. Overview
YouTube Plus RPC does not collect or store any personal data.

## 2. Data Processing
The following data is processed locally and never stored or shared:
- Video title and channel name (displayed in Discord status)
- Video thumbnail URL (displayed in Discord status)
- Playback time (used for progress display)

## 3. Third-Party Services
This app communicates with:
- **Discord** — to display Rich Presence status via Discord RPC
- **YouTube** — video data is read from the YouTube iOS app locally

We do not control the data practices of Discord or YouTube.

## 4. Data Storage
No user data is stored on any server. All processing happens locally on your device.

## 5. Children's Privacy
This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 6. Changes
We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page.

## 7. Contact
For privacy questions, contact us via GitHub Issues.
